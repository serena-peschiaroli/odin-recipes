Timbits

Description:

From Wikipedia: Timbits is the name of a bite-sized fried-dough confection sold at the Canadian-based franchise Tim Hortons. Equivalent to the American "donut hole," they were introduced in April 1976

Ingredients:

  • cups sifted flour, 2
  • Sugar, 1/3 cup
  • Baking Powder 3 tsp
  • Salt, ½ tsp
  • Egg, 1 beaten
  • Milk, ¾ cup
  • Oil, 3 tbs
  • Oil, for frying
  • Icing Sugar garnish

Steps:

  • Sift together dry ingredients
  • Mix together wet ingredients and incorporate into dry. Stir until smooth.
  • Drop by teaspoonfuls(no bigger) into hot oil (365 degrees, no hotter), turning after a few moments until golden brown on all sides.
  • Remove and drain.
  • Roll in cinnamon sugar while still warm and serve.
